Trouble accessing a course

If you are a Stanford student having trouble accessing ("Register for") a Stanford on-campus course:

- Log out of your OpenEdx account.
- Navigate to the "About" page of your course.
- Click on the big "Register" button, and you should be able to register for your course. You may be asked to authenticate with WebAuth first.
